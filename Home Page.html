Introduction

Skateboarding is an action sport that involves riding and performing various tricks, the trick in the picture on the right is called a tre flip (short for 360 flip), where the board is rotated 360 degrees while the board is flipped. The sport is a recreational activity that was invented during the late 1940s, where it was just a flat plank of wood with wheels; today it has developed technologies to help the skateboarder perform their tricks. One development is the nose and tail, where the tail allows the user to push down with their back foot and lift the board so that it can be set up for an ollie (a jump while on the skateboard) and the other foot slides up and is caught by the nose in order to stablise it.
